TEASEL STREET

BRANDING | BFA COURSE WORK

objective

Conceptualize and develop a brand identity for Teasel Street, a restaurant and bar located in the Rochester Public Market. The menu features seasonal, locally sourced interpretations of fusion cuisine. Teasel represents honest, hands–on, sociable food & drink served with a perfect blend of a unique food and warm social ambiance.
