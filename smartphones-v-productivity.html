SMARTPHONES

APPLICATION DESIGN | BFA COURSE WORK

objective

To learn to ethically conduct, analyze and visualize data. The final assignment was to create a poster based on two part research of personally collected data, surveys
and observation.

deliverables

➔ Primary data
➔ 36" x 48” Poster


approach

The topic of research was an opportunity to explore student relationships with their smartphones. Findings were represented in a visual way that responds to the pitfalls of increasingly accessible technology and the impact it has on educational productivity.
