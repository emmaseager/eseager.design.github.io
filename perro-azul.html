PERRO AZUL

APPLICATION DESIGN | BFA COURSE WORK

objective

Work made in Havana, Cuba, while participating in a two week design workshop with the students of their sole design school in the country, known as ISDi. The only given guideline: form a small team of four, identify an issue and devise a design solution.

deliverables

➔ Proposal Presentation
➔ Brand Materials
➔ Prototype
➔ Final Presentation

approach

Perro Azul is a structure created to aid the populous stray dogs of Old Havana by providing clean drinking water and shelter from the elements.

programs

➔ Adobe Illustrator
➔ SketchUp
